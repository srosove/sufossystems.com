Approach

1. Diagnose

Establish a clear baseline: governance health, operational risk, data/identity integrity, and modernization readiness.

2. Design

Define the modernization governance system: decision model, controls, reporting, and the minimum necessary tooling to sustain outcomes.

3. Operationalize

Embed governance into delivery and post go-live stewardship—so modernization remains stable, measurable, and improvable over time.
